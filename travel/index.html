2015 International Conference on Social Computing, Behavioral-Cultural Modeling, & Prediction (SBP15)
March 21 â€“ April 3, 2015, UCDC Center, Washington DC, USA

The 2015 International Conference on Social Computing, Behavioral-Cultural Modeling, and Prediction (SBP) 2013 will be held in downtown Washington, D.C. at the University of California DC Center (UCDC), 1608 Rhode Island Ave NW Washington, DC 20036, close to Farragut North, Farragut West, or Dupont Circle Metro stations.


View Larger Map

Metro: Take either the Red or Orange/Blue lines. From the Farragut North Metro station on the Red line or Farragut West on the Orange/Blue line, walk approximately 3 blocks north along 17th Street.

Driving: For driving directions to the Center at 1608 Rhode Island Avenue, NW, please click here.

Parking: There is street and garage parking nearby. An outdoor pay surface parking lot is available next door and there are covered garages in the surrounding blocks on M street and 17th Street. Nearby hotels such as the Beacon Hotel, Courtyard Washington Embassy Row, The Dupont Circle Hotel and The Jefferson provide valet parking to their guests.
